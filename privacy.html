Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At VaultDrop, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use VaultDrop, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information.

1. Information We Collect

Files Uploaded: VaultDrop does not permanently store the files you upload. Files are temporarily hosted on our secure servers solely for the purpose of transferring them to the intended recipient. Once the file expires (based on time or download count), or if it is manually deleted, it is permanently purged from our systems.

No Account Information: We do not require users to create an account, meaning we do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information for core functionality.

2. Data Security and Encryption

All files transferred through VaultDrop benefit from strong encryption. If you choose to set a password, the file is locked with that password, and VaultDrop administrators cannot access the content of your files.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising

VaultDrop may use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your user experience and for advertising purposes. You can choose to disable or selectively turn off our cookies or third-party cookies in your browser settings.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact page.
